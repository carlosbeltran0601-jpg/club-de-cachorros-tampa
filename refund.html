Refund Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At clubofpuppy.com, we strive to provide the best advertising platform for puppy lovers and breeders. Please read our refund policy carefully:

1. Membership Fees

The $20 monthly seller membership is a recurring subscription that covers the cost of hosting, verifying, and maintaining your puppy listings.

2. No Refunds

Because our service provides immediate access to our member network and listing tools, we generally do not offer refunds for partial months or for "change of mind" after a listing has been posted.

3. Cancellations

You may cancel your membership at any time. Once you cancel, your listings will remain active until the end of your current billing cycle, and you will not be charged again.

4. Contact Us

If you believe there has been a billing error or an unauthorized charge, please contact our support team immediately so we can resolve the issue.
